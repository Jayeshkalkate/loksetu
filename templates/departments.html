Government Departments

LokSetu connects citizens directly with the appropriate government departments responsible for resolving public service issues. By selecting the correct department, complaints can be processed faster and handled efficiently.

The platform improves transparency in public service management and helps authorities respond quickly to problems reported by citizens across villages and towns.

Public Service Departments

Public Utility Services

  • Water Supply Department
    Responsible for managing drinking water supply, pipeline maintenance, and water distribution across villages and towns.

  • Electricity Department
    Handles electricity services including power supply, street lighting maintenance, and repair of damaged electrical infrastructure.

Infrastructure & Development

  • Road & Infrastructure Department
    Responsible for construction and maintenance of roads, bridges, drainage systems, and other infrastructure facilities.

  • Sanitation Department
    Manages village cleanliness, garbage collection, drainage systems, and public sanitation services.

Social & Community Services

  • Health Department
    Responsible for public healthcare services including hospitals, health centers, vaccination programs, and medical facilities.

  • Education Department
    Oversees schools, educational infrastructure, teacher management, and development of learning facilities within villages.
