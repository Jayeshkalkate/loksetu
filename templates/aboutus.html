About LokSetu

Smart Public Complaint Tracker

LokSetu is a digital platform designed to improve communication between citizens and local government authorities. It allows people to easily submit Complaints related to public services such as water supply, electricity, sanitation, road maintenance, health facilities, and education. The platform helps ensure that problems are recorded properly and addressed by the responsible departments.

About Ranale Village

Ranale is a village located in the Nandurbar tehsil of Nandurbar district in the state of Maharashtra, India. The village is situated approximately 17 kilometers from both the Nandurbar tehsil and district headquarters. Ranale Gram Panchayat manages the local administration and development activities of the village.

According to the Census 2011 data, Ranale village has a total population of 7,403 residents, including 3,777 males and 3,626 females. The village has 1,620 households and covers a geographical area of about 3461.58 hectares. The pincode of Ranale is 425411.

Population and Literacy

The literacy rate of Ranale village is approximately 60.31%. Out of the total population, 4,465 people are literate while 2,938 people are illiterate. The village also has 856 children in the age group of 0–6 years. Scheduled Caste communities account for 415 residents and Scheduled Tribe communities account for 1,373 residents.

Transport and Connectivity

Ranale village has good connectivity to nearby towns and villages. Public bus services and private transport are available within the village. The nearest railway station is located more than 10 kilometers away. The road distance between Nandurbar city and Ranale village is approximately 17 kilometers, with an average travel time of 30 to 60 minutes depending on the mode of transport.

Our Mission

The main goal of LokSetu is to provide a transparent Complaint management system for villages and small towns. Citizens can report issues online, and the Complaints are forwarded directly to the concerned authorities. This helps reduce delays and improves the efficiency of public service management.

Technology Behind LokSetu

LokSetu is developed using modern web technologies such as Python, Django, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The platform can be integrated with cloud services and DevOps tools to improve performance, security, and scalability.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a smarter governance system where citizens can easily communicate with public departments. By using digital technology, LokSetu aims to support transparent governance and improve the quality of life in villages like Ranale and surrounding areas.
