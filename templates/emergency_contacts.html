Emergency Help

In case of emergencies, citizens can contact the following helpline numbers for immediate assistance. These services are available across Maharashtra and India.

Important Advice

For urgent situations such as accidents, fire hazards, medical emergencies, or law enforcement support, please use the official emergency numbers listed here.

Emergency Contact Numbers

National Emergency Helpline

  • 112 – National Emergency Helpline
  • 100 – Police
  • 101 – Fire Brigade
  • 102 – Ambulance
  • 108 – Emergency Medical Service

Women & Child Safety

  • 1091 – Women Helpline
  • 1098 – Child Helpline

Disaster Management

  • 1070 – State Disaster Management Helpline
  • 1077 – District Disaster Control Room

Health & Medical Services

  • 104 – Health Helpline
  • 108 – Emergency Medical Transport

Important Note

These emergency contact numbers are provided for the safety and assistance of citizens. For non-emergency public service complaints such as water supply, electricity, sanitation, or roads, please use the LokSetu complaint submission system.
