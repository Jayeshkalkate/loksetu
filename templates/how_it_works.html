About the Process

LokSetu makes it simple for citizens to report public issues and track their resolution online. The platform connects citizens with the concerned government departments in a transparent and efficient way.

Important Note

Make sure to provide correct information and a clear description of the issue while submitting a complaint. This helps the concerned department resolve the issue faster.

How LokSetu Works

Step 1: Register or Login

Citizens first need to create an account or login to the LokSetu platform. This helps maintain transparency and allows users to track their complaints easily.

Step 2: Submit a Complaint

After logging in, go to the Submit Complaint page. Fill in the complaint details such as the department, issue description, location, and any supporting information.

Step 3: Complaint is Assigned

Once the complaint is submitted, the LokSetu system forwards it to the concerned department and the responsible authority for review and action.

Step 4: Complaint Processing

The department reviews the complaint and begins the necessary steps to resolve the issue. Updates may be added during the resolution process.

Step 5: Track Complaint Status

Users can visit the Track Complaint page and enter their Complaint ID to check the current status and progress of their complaint.

Step 6: Issue Resolved

After the department resolves the issue, the complaint status is updated to Resolved. Citizens can view the final update and confirmation through the platform.
