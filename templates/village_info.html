About Ranala Village

Village Overview

Ranala is a developing village located in Maharashtra. The village is known for its strong community values, agricultural activities, and growing infrastructure. Local authorities continuously work to improve public services and living standards for residents.

Public Facilities

The village provides various facilities to support the daily needs of its citizens, including:

  • Government Schools
  • Primary Health Center
  • Water Supply System
  • Village Roads and Transportation
  • Electricity Supply
  • Sanitation Services

Role of LokSetu

LokSetu is designed to improve communication between citizens and village authorities. Through this platform, residents can easily report problems related to public services and track their Complaint status online.

Future Development

The village administration aims to improve infrastructure, digital governance, and citizen services. Platforms like LokSetu help ensure transparency, accountability, and faster resolution of community issues.
