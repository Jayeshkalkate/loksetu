Usage Guidelines

LokSetu is a digital platform designed to help citizens report public issues and track their resolution. Users must follow the guidelines and submit complaints responsibly.

Important Notice

Terms & Conditions

1. Acceptance of Terms

By accessing or using the LokSetu platform, users agree to comply with these Terms and Conditions. If you do not agree with these terms, you should not use the platform.

2. Purpose of the Platform

LokSetu is intended to help citizens report issues related to public services such as water supply, roads, electricity, sanitation, health, and education. The platform aims to improve communication between citizens and government departments.

3. User Responsibilities

Users must provide accurate and truthful information while submitting complaints. Misuse of the platform, including submitting false complaints or offensive content, is strictly prohibited.

4. Complaint Handling

Complaints submitted through LokSetu are forwarded to the concerned departments for review and action. Resolution time may vary depending on the nature of the issue and the responsible department.

5. Limitation of Liability

LokSetu provides a platform for reporting issues but does not guarantee immediate resolution of every complaint. The responsible departments handle the final action and resolution.

6. Prohibited Activities

Users must not use the platform for unlawful activities, including submitting fake complaints, harassment, spam, or any form of misuse that disrupts the service.

7. Changes to Terms

LokSetu reserves the right to update or modify these Terms & Conditions at any time. Continued use of the platform indicates acceptance of the updated terms.

8. Contact Information

If you have any questions regarding these Terms & Conditions, please contact the LokSetu support team through the Contact page.
