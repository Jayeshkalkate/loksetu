Village Services

LokSetu provides a digital platform where citizens can report issues related to village services and track the progress of their complaints.

The platform helps connect villagers directly with responsible departments, improving transparency and faster problem resolution.

Citizen Feedback

“LokSetu makes it easy for villagers to report problems like road damage and water supply issues. The response from authorities has become much faster.” - Village Resident

Village Services

  • Public Service Department

    Water Supply Management

    Citizens can report issues related to drinking water, pipeline leakage, and water supply interruptions through the LokSetu complaint system.

  • Public Utility Department

    Electricity & Street Lights

    LokSetu allows villagers to report electricity outages, damaged poles, or non-functioning street lights to the responsible department.

  • Infrastructure Department

    Roads & Infrastructure

    Citizens can report potholes, damaged roads, drainage problems, and other infrastructure issues through LokSetu.
