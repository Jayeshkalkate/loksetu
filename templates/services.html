Village Public Services

LokSetu is a digital governance platform that enables citizens to report issues related to public services in their local areas. The system helps ensure that complaints are directed to the appropriate government departments.

By using LokSetu, citizens can easily communicate with local authorities and track the progress of their complaints, improving transparency and faster problem resolution.

Public Service Categories

  • Public Water Department

    Water Supply Services

    Citizens can report issues related to drinking water supply, pipeline leakage, water shortage, or irregular water distribution through the LokSetu complaint system.

  • Electricity & Utility Department

    Electricity & Street Lighting

    Villagers can report electricity outages, damaged electric poles, or malfunctioning street lights so the responsible department can take action quickly.

  • Infrastructure & Development Department

    Roads & Infrastructure

    Citizens can submit complaints related to potholes, damaged roads, drainage issues, and other infrastructure problems affecting their village or town.
