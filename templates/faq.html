Help & Support

LokSetu is designed to help citizens report public service issues quickly and easily. Through this platform, users can submit complaints related to village infrastructure and track their status online.

If your question is not answered in the FAQ section, please reach out to the LokSetu support team for assistance.

Quick Tip

After submitting a complaint, you will receive a unique Complaint ID. Keep this ID safe as it will help you track the progress and updates of your complaint.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is LokSetu?

LokSetu is a smart digital platform that allows citizens to report public issues related to village services. It connects citizens directly with the responsible departments so that complaints can be addressed efficiently and transparently.

2. How can I submit a complaint?

To submit a complaint, navigate to the Submit Complaint page on the website. Fill in the required information, select the appropriate department, describe your issue clearly, and submit the form.

3. How can I track my complaint?

Once your complaint is submitted, you will receive a unique Complaint ID. Use this ID on the Track Complaint page to check the current progress and status of your complaint.

4. Which departments are available on LokSetu?

LokSetu supports complaints related to several government and village departments, including:

  • Water Supply Department
  • Electricity Department
  • Roads and Infrastructure
  • Sanitation and Waste Management
  • Health Services
  • Education Services

5. How long does it take to resolve a complaint?

The resolution time may vary depending on the type of issue and the department responsible for handling it. LokSetu helps speed up the process by sending complaints directly to the concerned authorities.

6. Who can use LokSetu?

LokSetu is designed for all citizens living in villages or rural areas of Maharashtra. Any user can submit and track complaints related to public services through the platform.
