Help & Support

The LokSetu platform helps citizens easily report issues related to village services and track their Complaint status online.

If you do not find the answer to your question below, please contact the LokSetu support team.

Quick Tip

Always keep your Complaint ID safe. You will need it to track the progress of your Complaint.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is LokSetu?

LokSetu is a Smart Public Complaint Tracker that allows citizens to report issues related to village services such as water supply, electricity, sanitation, roads, education, and health facilities.

2. How can I submit a Complaint?

To submit a Complaint, go to the "Submit Complaint" page, fill in the required details, select the relevant department, and submit your Complaint.

3. How can I track my Complaint?

After submitting a Complaint, you will receive a Complaint ID. You can use this ID on the "Track Complaint" page to check the current status of your Complaint.

4. Which departments are available on LokSetu?

LokSetu supports Complaints related to multiple departments such as:

  • Water Supply
  • Electricity
  • Roads & Infrastructure
  • Sanitation
  • Health Services
  • Education

5. How long does it take to resolve a Complaint?

The resolution time depends on the type of issue and the department responsible. However, the LokSetu system helps departments respond faster by directly forwarding Complaints to them.

6. Who can use LokSetu?

Any citizen of the village or nearby area can use LokSetu to report public service issues and track Complaint updates.
