Privacy Commitment

LokSetu respects the privacy of its users. We are committed to protecting the personal information shared by citizens while using the platform.

Data Protection

All information submitted through LokSetu is used only for complaint management and service improvement. We do not sell or misuse user data.

Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

LokSetu is a digital platform designed to help citizens report public service issues and track their resolution. This privacy policy explains how user information is collected, used, and protected while using the LokSetu platform.

2. Information We Collect

LokSetu may collect the following types of information when users submit complaints or create accounts:

  • Name and contact information
  • Email address or phone number
  • Complaint details and location information
  • Department related to the complaint

3. How We Use the Information

The information collected is used strictly for the purpose of managing and resolving public complaints. It helps the system forward complaints to the appropriate department and keep citizens updated about the status of their issues.

4. Data Security

LokSetu takes appropriate technical and administrative measures to protect user information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. Access to complaint data is limited to authorized administrators and departments.

5. Information Sharing

LokSetu does not share personal user information with third parties except when necessary to forward complaints to the responsible government departments for resolution.

6. User Responsibility

Users are responsible for providing accurate information when submitting complaints. False or misleading information may delay the complaint resolution process.

7. Updates to This Policy

LokSetu may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect improvements in the system or legal requirements. Users are encouraged to review this page periodically.
