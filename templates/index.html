The LokSetu Vision

LokSetu is a digital public service platform designed to connect citizens with government authorities across Maharashtra. The system allows residents to report public issues and access important government information through a simple and transparent online portal.

Citizens can submit complaints by selecting their District, Taluka, and Village, ensuring that the issue is directed to the correct local authority. The platform helps government departments monitor and resolve problems more efficiently while keeping citizens informed about the status of their complaints.

Key Features of LokSetu

  • Easy Complaint Submission
    Citizens can easily report issues related to public services such as roads, water supply, electricity, sanitation, healthcare, and education from anywhere in Maharashtra.

  • Department-Based Resolution
    Each complaint is automatically directed to the relevant government department responsible for resolving the issue.

  • Real-Time Complaint Tracking
    After submitting a complaint, citizens receive a Complaint ID that allows them to track the progress and resolution status online.

  • Stronger Citizen–Government Communication
    LokSetu strengthens communication between citizens and government authorities, helping ensure faster issue resolution and better public service management.
