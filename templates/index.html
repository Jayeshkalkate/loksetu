The LokSetu Promise

LokSetu is a Smart Public Complaint Tracker designed to improve transparency between citizens and government departments across Maharashtra. It allows people to report issues related to public services such as water supply, electricity, sanitation, roads, healthcare, and education.

The system ensures that Complaints are properly recorded and forwarded to the responsible department. Citizens can submit Complaints by selecting their District, Taluka, and Village, and track the progress of their Complaint online.

Why Use LokSetu?

  • Submit Complaints Easily Citizens can report public service issues from any district, taluka, or village in Maharashtra using the online Complaint system.

  • Department Based System Complaints are automatically forwarded to the appropriate government department responsible for resolving the issue.

  • Track Complaint Status Citizens can track the progress of their Complaint using the Complaint ID provided after submission.

  • Citizen Support LokSetu strengthens communication between citizens and authorities, helping ensure faster problem resolution.
